About Us

Bhavani Pickles was born during one of the most difficult phases of life. During the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, when the world was facing uncertainty and loss, our family also experienced a deep personal tragedy with the loss of my husband. Coping with grief and emotional hardship without the support of extended family or friends was incredibly difficult. In that period of loneliness and healing, the idea of starting something meaningful took shape. Preparing traditional homemade pickles—something that had always been part of our kitchen and culture—became a way to stay strong, find purpose, and slowly overcome depression.

From that small beginning came Bhavani Pickles, a heartfelt effort to share authentic, homemade flavors with others. Our goal is to bring the taste of traditional, homemade pickles to people who cherish genuine, comforting food made with care and love. Each pickle is prepared with carefully selected ingredients and traditional methods to ensure rich flavor and quality. What started as a personal journey of healing has now become a mission to deliver delicious, homemade pickles that remind people of home, warmth, and tradition.
