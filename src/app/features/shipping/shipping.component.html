Shipping, Cancellation & Refund Policy

inventory Order Preparation

Once an order is successfully placed on our website, it is sent for preparation and packing. Orders are usually prepared and dispatched within 1–3 business days, excluding weekends and public holidays.

After the order has been shipped, customers will receive shipping confirmation along with tracking details.

local_shipping Delivery & Shipping

Delivery Time

Delivery time may vary depending on the delivery location and the courier partner's availability. Estimated delivery timelines are:

schedule
Standard Delivery 5–8 business days
bolt
Express Delivery 2–4 business days (if available)

info Delays may occasionally occur due to logistics issues, weather conditions, holidays, or remote delivery locations.

Shipping Charges

  • Shipping fees are calculated automatically during checkout based on the delivery location and order value.
  • Promotional offers such as free shipping on certain order values may be available from time to time.

cancel Order Cancellation

  • Customers can request cancellation within a limited time after placing the order, before it is dispatched from our facility.
  • Once the order has been packed or handed over to the courier service, cancellation requests cannot be accepted.
  • To cancel an order, please contact our support team with your order details as soon as possible.

assignment_return Returns

Since our products are food items and meant for consumption, we generally do not accept returns after the product has been delivered.

However, if a customer receives:

  • A damaged product
  • A wrong item
  • A product that is not in good condition

They must notify us within 24 hours of receiving the order. Customers may be asked to provide photos or proof of the issue so our team can review the case.

payments Refunds

  • If a complaint is verified and approved, we will initiate a refund or replacement, depending on the situation.
  • Refunds will be processed back to the original mode of payment and may take 5–7 business days to reflect, depending on the payment provider.
  • Refunds may also be issued in case of undelivered or lost shipments after verification with the courier partner.

location_on Address & Delivery Responsibility

  • Customers are responsible for entering the correct shipping address and contact information at the time of placing the order.
  • If an order is returned due to incorrect address details or unsuccessful delivery attempts, the customer may be required to pay additional shipping charges for re-dispatch.

support_agent Contact Support

For any questions related to orders, delivery, cancellations, or refunds, please contact our support team.
