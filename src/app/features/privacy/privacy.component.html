Privacy Policy

At Bhavani Pickles, we value the trust you place in our home-made traditions. This policy explains how we look after your information when you shop for our fresh and tasty pickles.

info How We Get Your Information

When you visit our site or buy a jar of pickles, we receive information in two ways:

  • Details You Give Us: This includes your name, phone number, and address so we can deliver your order.
  • Automatic Details: Our website uses cookies to remember things like your Wishlist or what's in your cart to make your shopping experience smooth.

thumb_up Using Your Information for Good

We only use your data to make sure you get the best service:

  • Shipping Your Pickles: We use your address to send our products to your home in Hyderabad or across India.
  • Quick Support: If you message us on WhatsApp, we use your contact info to solve your queries fast.
  • Website Improvements: We look at how people use the site to make it easier to find your favorite flavors.

handshake Sharing with Care

We treat your data like a secret family recipe — we never sell or rent it. We only share it with:

  • Delivery Partners: To get the package to your door.
  • Secure Payment Apps: To handle your money safely using industry-standard protection.
  • Legal Requirements: Only if the law strictly requires us to do so.

shield Your Safety & Your Rights

  • Safe Storage: We use strong security to protect your private details from being lost or stolen.
  • Your Choice: You can ask us to show you what data we have, update your phone number, or delete your account at any time.
  • Cookies: You can turn off cookies in your browser, but the site might not remember your favorite pickles for your next visit.

update Updates to This Policy

As our kitchen grows, we might update this page. We encourage you to check back whenever you visit to see how we are protecting you.
